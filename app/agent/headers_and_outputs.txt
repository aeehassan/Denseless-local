**Recovery Time Objective (RTO):** The maximum acceptable duration for an IT system or application to be unavailable after a disaster or failure. It defines the target for how quickly systems must be restored.

**Recovery Point Objective (RPO):** The maximum acceptable amount of data loss, measured in time, that can occur after a disaster or failure. It dictates how frequently data backups or replication must occur.

**Key Distinction:**
*   **RTO is about TIME:** How *long* can we be down?
*   **RPO is about DATA:** How *much* data can we afford to lose?
